## Purpose

This is where you think out loud. Journal entries, meeting notes, daily observations - anything that captures your thought process.

**This directory is gitignored by default.** Your reflections are private. If you want to share some entries, move them to `2-Knowledge/` or adjust the `.gitignore`.

## What Goes Here

- Daily/weekly reflections
- Meeting notes
- Observations and patterns you're noticing
- Ideas that aren't ready to be formalized

## Structure

Organize however makes sense for you. Common patterns:
- By date: `2026-04-07-reflection.md`
- By week: `Week-15-2026.md`
- By topic: `meetings/`, `reflections/`

Your AI will help you find the right structure during training.
