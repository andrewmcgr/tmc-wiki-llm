## Content

After 3 weeks of killing starters, finally found a rhythm that works:

- **Room temperature (68-72°F):** Feed 1:1:1 (starter:flour:water) every 12 hours
- **Fridge storage:** Feed once, let rise 2 hours at room temp, then refrigerate. Feed once a week.
- **Before baking:** Take out of fridge, feed twice over 12 hours to wake it up. Should double in size within 4-6 hours when ready.

The float test actually works - drop a spoonful in water, if it floats it's ready to bake with.
